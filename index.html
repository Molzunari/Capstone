Mysterious birds

Welcome to the house of aviary action!


These majestic birds have graced the skies for millions of years. They have evolved from therapods, so while
these birds aren't as fierce as their ancestors, they can still pack a wallop! At Mysterious Birds, we have a wide selection
of birds ranging from the barn owl, toco toucan, macaw, to small birds like canaries and finches. Our prices
are fairly reasonable, so it won't put much stress on those on a budget. I'd love to give these birds away to all
of you wonderful people, so please, come see these beautiful birds for yourself!


However, I want to inform everyone here that these birds that I have in stock are not the only kinds of birds
that I have at the moment. I have carefully selected a good handful of upcoming birds that will arrive in the near
future. If you would like to purchase of these birds in stock, they require a huge amount of space. They get rather
irritated easily if they are confined to a cage for a set amount of time. Each of the birds' diet vary, so please keep
that in mind. That being said, it would be an absolute treat once you come in. Take care everyone!


Types of birds on stock:

  • Diurnal Birds of Prey (Accipitriformes)
  • Parrots (Psittaciformes)
